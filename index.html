Data is our north star. We leverage proprietary algorithms and real-world datasets to match global therapeutic assets with the specific unmet needs of the Chinese patient population — and to design trial strategies that maximize NMPA approval probability.

/ Company Story

Navigating the future of
cross-border biotech.

A bridge that exists nowhere else.

BioArch was formed at the intersection of a simple observation and an untapped opportunity: the United States produces more biomedical innovation than any country in history, and China has quietly built one of the world's most sophisticated clinical infrastructure networks — anchored by Hainan Lecheng's real-world study pathway, which can move a foreign-approved drug from filing to Chinese patients in nine months.

Most US biotechs have no operating presence in China and no way to build the relationships needed to access this infrastructure within a reasonable deal timeline. Most Chinese intermediaries lack the scientific depth to credibly evaluate complex modalities like siRNA, cell therapy, or novel GLP-1 combinations. BioArch was built to close both gaps simultaneously.

Our model is deliberately light.

We are a lean partnership, not a consulting factory. Every engagement is handled by the partners whose names are on this page — not delegated to analysts, not managed through a ticketing system. We take a small number of engagements at a time, and we choose them on the basis of scientific merit, pathway fit, and team conviction.

Our operating model is structured as a US LLC and a Hainan limited partnership — allowing us to act as legal representatives in Lecheng while operating commercially from the United States. We are not brokers. We are not finders. We are operators, advisors, and connectors — with skin in the outcome.
